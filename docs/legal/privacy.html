Privacy Policy

Side Quest (“we”, “us”) is operated by Engine Labs. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Side Quest mobile app.

Information we collect

  • Account data: phone number, email, or OAuth profile identifiers when you sign in (via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile data: display name and optional profile details you provide.
  • Location: device location only while you use venue check-in features, to show nearby venues and validate check-in.
  • Activity: venue check-ins, connection requests, and in-app messages with other users at the same venue.
  • Safety reports: reports you submit about other users, including the reason you select.

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • Show venues near you and manage venue rooms.
  • Enable messaging and connection requests between checked-in users.
  • Enforce safety features (blocking, reporting, moderation).
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We use service providers to operate the app:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and realtime messaging.
  • Google / Apple — optional sign-in providers you choose.
  • SMS provider (e.g. Twilio via Supabase) — phone verification codes.

Other users at your venue may see your display name and profile information you choose to share while you are checked in.

Retention

We retain account and activity data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us. Some records may be kept where required for safety, legal, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Your choices

  • Deny location permission — you cannot check in to venues, but may still browse sign-in options.
  • Block users — blocked users cannot message you or appear in your room.
  • Sign out or delete your account — contact privacy@enginelabs.au.

Children

Side Quest is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to production systems is restricted. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the revised policy.
